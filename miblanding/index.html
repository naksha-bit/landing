How to Apply

  • Applications will be shortlisted by IMS Ghaziabad on the basis of graduation merit.

  • Shortlisted candidates will be asked to appear for GD / PI.Admissions as per University Guidelines.

Eligibility Criteria


  • Graduation from recognized university with minimum aggregate of 50%.

    • Or


  • Candidates appearing for the qualifying / aptitude examination in 2019 / 2020 (Or whose results are awaited can also apply).

Documents Required

  • Photocopy of X & XII mark sheets (With Original)
  • Photocopy of Passing Certificates (With Original)
  • Photocopy of Migration Certificate (With Original). Not required if the candidate has done his/her graduation from CCS University, Meerut.
  • Recent Passport size photographs
  • Gap Year Certificate (In case Gap of 1 or 2 years after Graduation).

Why MIB?

To create cutting edge professionals to handle International Business environment for domestic and multi-national organizations.
If you can imagine yourself in negotiating, strategizing or leading a major company in the global arena then masters in International Business is the key to your future dreams. The MIB program offers opportunities to learn global business techniques, process, and models and acquire skills to apply them. The students are prepared to handle various markets, cultures and products in international environment.

MIB Placement Report 2019

The campus placement process for Masters in International Business (MIB) of batch 2017-19 has been remarkable with over 83% of the students already placed, 8% have opted for family business as on 27th Feb 2019. The placement season saw participation from major recruiters like Aditya Birla Fashion Retail, Coffee Day Beverages, Nestle India, Oyo Rooms, Berger Paints, S&P Global, Protiviti Consulting, Ameriprise Financial, E&Y, Deki Electronics to name a few.

Major Highlights

  • More than 50 companies shared Final Placement Job Profiles with MIB batch
  • Participation of companies from various sectors such as BFSI, IT/ITES, Consulting, E-Commerce, FMCG, Real Estate Developers, IB and others
  • 20% of the students grabbed Multiple offers with an average CTC of 7.77 LPA
  • 17% of the students got offers from International Organisations
  • Top 50% of the batch got placed with average CTC of 10.57 LPA
  • Sector-wise highest job profiles offered were in BFSI/KPO (21%) followed by IT/ITES (18%), FMCG/Consumer Durable (14%), Ecommerce (11%), Advertisement/Event Management (9%), Real Estate (9%), Logistic & Supply Chain Management (7%)
  • Highest CTC offered was 16.0 LPA and Average CTC offered was 7.53 LPA
